# 02 - Brand Voice and Landing Copy

## Voice Direction

- Playful, internet-native, and lightly chaotic
- Not corporate, not clinical
- Funny without being mean

## Hero Copy Options

Primary recommendation:

- **"Your AI has tea about you."**
- Subhead: **"Let it rate your work style and reveal your type."**

Alternatives:

- "Wanna know what your AI really thinks of you?"
- "You think you're clear. Your AI might disagree."
- "Find your agent-eye personality type in 2 minutes."

## CTA Options

- "Read Me, Robot"
- "Run My Agent Test"
- "Show Me My Type"

## Mode Labels

Current recommendation:

- `Normal Mode`
- `Intrusive Thoughts Mode`

Fallback labels:

- `Normal` / `Spicy`
- `Professional` / `Unfiltered`
- `Calm` / `Chaotic`

## Tone Rule

Every joke should still feel like "we're in this together," not "the product is insulting you."
